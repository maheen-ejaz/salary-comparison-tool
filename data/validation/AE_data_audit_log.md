## 1. Salary Bands Verification

### Sources Consulted
- [Doctor Salary in UAE 2025 - Academically](https://academically.com/blogs/doctor-salary-in-uae/)
- [Doctor Salary Dubai 2025 Complete Guide - Doctoflow](https://doctoflow.com/en/doctor-salary-dubai-complete-guide-for-2025-earn-up-to-150-000-aed-tax-free/)
- [Dubai Healthcare Specialist Salary Guide 2025](https://www.allocationassist.com/dubai-healthcare-specialist-salary-guide/)
- [Indian Doctors in Dubai Salaries Jobs 2026](https://terratern.com/blog/indian-doctors-in-dubai/)

### Salary Data Confidence Level
- **House Officer / Junior Doctor:** MEDIUM — Limited public data available. Entry-level salaries (AED 4,000-8,000/month) based on reports from Indian doctor communities and recruitment portals. Variation depends heavily on emirate and hospital.
- **Medical Officer:** HIGH — Well-documented range (AED 10,000-18,000/month). Consistent across multiple sources for doctors with 2-5 years experience.
- **Specialist:** HIGH — Strong consensus (AED 30,000-60,000/month) across multiple authoritative sources. Variations exist by specialty (cardiology, radiology pay higher).
- **Consultant:** MEDIUM-HIGH — Range (AED 70,000-110,000/month) is widely cited, but top-tier private hospital consultants can exceed this significantly, especially with revenue-sharing arrangements.

### Data Conflicts & Resolution
- **Conflict:** Some sources report House Officer salaries as low as AED 2,000-3,000/month for UAE internship year.
  - **Resolution:** These figures refer to the mandatory 1-year internship before licensing, not post-license employment. Our data reflects post-DHA/DOH/MOH license salaries.

- **Conflict:** Consultant salary upper bounds vary from AED 100,000 to AED 200,000+/month.
  - **Resolution:** We used AED 110,000/month as the conservative "max" to reflect salaried positions. Revenue-sharing consultants at premium hospitals (Mediclinic, NMC, Burjeel) can earn significantly more but this is not guaranteed.

### Notes on Salary Structure
- **Housing Allowance:** Typically 20-40% of base salary, paid separately. NOT included in our salary figures to maintain consistency with other countries in the tool.
- **Transport Allowance:** AED 1,000-3,000/month common, also separate.
- **Annual Bonuses:** Performance-based bonuses (5-15% of annual salary) common in private sector but not guaranteed.
- **Sector Premium:** Private hospitals generally pay 10-20% more than government facilities for equivalent positions.

## 2. Tax Rules Verification

### UAE Tax Structure for Expatriates
**Verified:** UAE has NO personal income tax for expatriate workers as of 2026.

### Sources
- [Tax-Free Salary in UAE - Incorportas](https://www.incorportas.com/blog-posts/tax-free-salary-uae)
- [Taxes in UAE 2026 Ultimate Guide - Global Citizen Solutions](https://www.globalcitizensolutions.com/taxes-in-uae/)
- [No Income Tax - Ministry of Economy & Tourism UAE](https://www.moet.gov.ae/en/-/no-income-tax-and-full-profit-transfer)

### Key Points
- **Income Tax:** 0% for all expatriates (doctors included)
- **Social Security:** UAE nationals contribute 5% employee + 12.5% employer. Expatriates are EXEMPT.
- **Health Insurance:** Mandatory as of 2025 for all visa holders. Typically employer-sponsored (deducted from salary or provided as benefit). Cost: AED 500-1,000/month if self-paid.
- **Pension/Retirement:** No mandatory contributions for expats. Some employers offer gratuity (21 days salary per year of service after 1 year, 30 days after 5 years) as end-of-service benefit.

### Utilities & Transport
- **DEWA (Dubai Electricity & Water Authority):** Verified average AED 400-800/month depending on apartment size and usage.

- **Dining Out:** Budget meal AED 20-30, mid-range restaurant AED 60-100, fine dining AED 150-300 per person.

### Healthcare
- **Set to AED 0** in all cost-of-living scenarios because health insurance is mandatory and employer-sponsored in 95%+ of medical employment contracts.

### Items Requiring Re-Verification (Future Updates)
- **School Fees:** Widely cited as AED 20,000-120,000/year depending on curriculum (Indian CBSE schools AED 20,000-40,000, British/American international schools AED 60,000-120,000). NOT included in Family Premium tier — too variable. Recommend adding note in UI.

## 8. Disclaimer & Limitations

- **Date Sensitivity:** UAE job market and licensing requirements change frequently. Salary data valid as of Feb 2026; verify annually.
- **Hospital-Specific Variation:** Actual offers vary significantly by hospital chain (NMC vs. Thumbay vs. government hospitals), specialty, and negotiation.
- **Licensing Body Differences:** DHA (Dubai), DOH (Abu Dhabi), and MOH (Northern Emirates) have different exam formats, fees, and timelines. Our data generalizes across all three.
- **Housing Allowances:** Not included in salary figures but typically add 20-40% to total compensation. Users should be informed of this.
- **School Fees:** Not included in Premium Family tier due to extreme variability (INR 5-30 lakh/year per child).
